The Dream (the list)

Chapter 2 of This Old Boat by Don Casey The Dream talks about defining why you have the boat. What's your dream day on the water like? Then it directs you to write down a list of everything needed to be done to the boat to achieve this dream.

The dream

A pretty boat, easy to sail, comfortable for day cruises and overnight getaways on the river, and capable of going for three or four nights as far as Busselton or Rottnest.
